Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data. While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide Us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify You, including:

  • Email address and message contents if You contact support via form or email.
  • Optional information You may choose to provide in Your message (e.g., name, device model, OS version).

Workout & Progression Data. When You log workouts in the App (e.g., exercises, sets, reps, weights, rest times, notes), this information is stored locally on Your Device and is not transmitted to Our servers.

Usage Data. When using the Service, anonymous diagnostics such as crash logs may be provided by Apple to help Us improve performance and stability. If You enable a music integration (e.g., Spotify), the App may request limited playback permissions strictly to control in-app audio; We do not access Your Spotify password or personal listening history.

Tracking Technologies and Cookies

The Application does not use cookies. The support Website may use minimal Cookies (e.g., to remember cookie consent). You can instruct Your browser to refuse Cookies; some Website features may not function without them.

Use of Your Personal Data

The Company may use Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service, including enabling workout logging and progression tracking on Your Device.
  • To contact You to respond to support requests You submit.
  • To manage Your requests and improve customer support.
  • To improve the Service using anonymous diagnostics and crash reports.
  • For legal compliance and to protect against fraud or abuse.
We do not sell or rent Your Personal Data.

Sharing Your Personal Data

We may share limited information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers that process support submissions (e.g., Formspree) solely to operate the contact form and deliver Your message to Us.
  • For business transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (You will be notified before any change of controller).
  • For legal reasons to comply with law or valid requests by public authorities.
  • With Your consent for any other purpose disclosed at the time of collection.

Retention of Your Personal Data

  • Workout & progression data remain on Your Device until You delete them or uninstall the App (unless preserved in device backups).
  • Support messages are retained in Our support inbox for as long as necessary to resolve Your request and for limited archival, unless You request deletion.
  • Anonymous diagnostics are retained by Apple under its own policies.

Transfer of Your Personal Data

We do not transfer Your workout/progression data to Our servers. Contact submissions may be processed by Service Providers or email providers that operate in other countries; We take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate protections are in place.

Delete Your Personal Data

You may delete workouts and progression history from within the App at any time. Uninstalling the App removes locally stored data (subject to device backups). To request deletion of support messages You sent to Us, contact support@ampdfit.app.

Business Transactions. If the Company is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, Your Personal Data may be transferred. We will provide notice before Your Personal Data is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.
