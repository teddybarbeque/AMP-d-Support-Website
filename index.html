Quick help

Connect Spotify
  1. Open Amp’d → Settings → Music.
  2. Tap “Connect Spotify” and approve the permissions.
  3. Return to Amp’d. Pick playlists for Sets and Rests.
Set rest timers

Choose a workout type on the home screen. Amp’d suggests rest times for compounds vs accessories. Fine-tune in Settings → Timers.

Restore purchase

If you’ve paid and it’s not unlocked, go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you’re on the same Apple ID.
